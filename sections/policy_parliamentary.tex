\section{Policy, Parliamentary and Public Affairs Research}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep]
    \item Conducted in‑depth policy research on governance, public finance, welfare delivery, election law, and federalism, drafting submission‑ready parliamentary questions, Zero Hour submissions, motions, and brief notes.
    \item Analyzed statutes, parliamentary rules, ECI circulars, court orders, committee reports, and government records to deliver evidence‑backed arguments and strategic briefs for senior decision‑makers.
    \item Spearheaded drafting and research support for the \textit{Aparajita Bill, 2024} (crimes against women and children) and formulated legal‑policy frameworks for the \textit{Global Capability Centre (GCC) Policy, West Bengal 2024}.

    \item Evaluated school‑infrastructure deficits and Samagra Shiksha implementation, drafting policy briefs correlating welfare delivery with public expenditure.
    \item Synthesized large volumes of fiscal, regulatory, and legal data to author expert submissions, briefs, newsletters, and op‑eds for diverse public and institutional audiences.
